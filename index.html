BURN'D

this is a safe place to say it.

There are things we carry around for years. Words we never sent. Conversations that never happened. Things we wish we'd said — to someone else, or to ourselves.

Write it here. All of it. No one will read it. No one will know. When you're ready, burn it — and leave a little lighter than you came.

it's gone. and so is some of what you were carrying.
